# Product Mission

## Pitch

**Speak Pho Real** is a Vietnamese language learning application that helps language learners develop conversational fluency through real-time AI-powered voice practice with a native-speaking tutor persona ("Co Ha"). Unlike traditional language apps that focus on vocabulary drills and reading comprehension, Speak Pho Real provides authentic conversation practice with immediate feedback, corrections, and cultural context.

## Users

### Primary Customers

- **Heritage Speakers**: Vietnamese diaspora seeking to reconnect with their language and culture
- **Language Enthusiasts**: People learning Vietnamese for travel, relationships, or personal enrichment
- **Expats & Travelers**: Individuals planning to live in or visit Vietnam who need practical conversational skills
- **Students**: Formal Vietnamese language students supplementing classroom learning

## The Problem

### Language Learning Lacks Speaking Practice

The biggest barrier to language fluency is speaking practice. Traditional apps focus on reading, writing, and listening, but speaking requires a conversation partner. Human tutors are expensive ($30-50/hour), require scheduling, and availability for Vietnamese is limited.

**Quantifiable Impact**: 80% of language learners cite "lack of speaking practice" as their primary obstacle. The average learner needs 600+ hours of conversation practice to reach fluency.

**Our Solution**: AI-powered voice conversations available 24/7, providing unlimited speaking practice at a fraction of the cost of human tutors, with the patience to correct every mistake without judgment.

### Vietnamese Has Unique Learning Challenges

Vietnamese is a tonal language with six tones that completely change word meanings. Mispronunciation leads to confusion or embarrassment. Traditional learning resources do not provide real-time feedback on tone production.

**Our Solution**: Real-time AI voice interaction that understands tonal variations and provides immediate corrections with explanations of what went wrong and how to improve.

## Differentiators

### Real-Time Voice Conversation

Unlike text-based chatbots or pre-recorded lessons, we provide live voice conversations using advanced AI (Gemini Live API / OpenAI Realtime API). Users speak naturally and receive immediate spoken responses, mimicking real conversation flow.

**Result**: Users develop actual speaking skills rather than just recognition abilities.

### Dual Practice Modes

**Free Chat Mode**: Natural conversation without interruptions - build confidence and fluency through immersion.

**Coach Mode**: Active correction and teaching - every mistake is identified, corrected, and explained with cultural context.

**Result**: Users can choose their learning style and progress at their own pace.

### Provider Resilience

Automatic fallback from Gemini to OpenAI ensures sessions are never lost to connection issues. Conversation history is preserved across provider switches.

**Result**: Reliable learning experience without technical frustrations.

### Topic-Based Learning

Pre-selected conversation topics (Food, Travel, Family, Work, Hobbies, Shopping, Culture) allow focused practice on relevant vocabulary and situations.

**Result**: Users can prepare for specific real-world scenarios they will encounter.

## Key Features

### Core Features

- **AI Voice Conversations**: Real-time spoken dialogue with AI tutor "Co Ha" using natural language understanding
- **Practice Mode Selection**: Choose between Free Chat (fluency) and Coach Mode (accuracy)
- **Topic Selection**: Focus conversations on specific themes relevant to user goals
- **Difficulty Levels**: Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced settings adjust vocabulary and speech complexity

### Learning Features

- **Real-Time Corrections**: Coach mode provides immediate feedback on grammar, tone, vocabulary, and word order
- **Correction Explanations**: Each correction includes cultural and linguistic context
- **Session Summaries**: End-of-session review of all corrections and practice points
- **Learning History**: Persistent storage of past conversations and corrections for review

### Platform Features

- **Usage Tracking**: Real-time minute tracking with monthly allowances based on subscription tier
- **Provider Fallback**: Seamless switch between Gemini and OpenAI if connection issues occur
- **Session Recovery**: State preservation if browser crashes or connection drops

## Success Metrics

### User Engagement

- Sessions per user per week
- Average session duration
- Topic diversity (variety of topics practiced)
- Mode usage distribution (Free Chat vs Coach)

### Learning Progress

- Corrections per session (trending downward over time indicates improvement)
- Correction categories (tracking which areas need most work)
- User self-reported confidence levels

### Business Health

- Monthly Active Users (MAU)
- Free to Paid conversion rate
- Subscription retention rate
- Net Promoter Score (NPS)

### Technical Performance

- Session completion rate (sessions ended normally vs errors)
- Provider switch rate (Gemini failures requiring OpenAI fallback)
- Average session latency
